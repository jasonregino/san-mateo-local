A quiet tech hometown

You've used more San Mateo than you think.

This town looks like parks, taquerias, and Little League games, and it is. It's also where a surprising slice of the internet got its start, and where a lot of it still runs from today.

Started here

YouTube
The whole thing began in 2005, in an office above Amici's pizzeria on East 3rd Avenue.
Electronic Arts
Trip Hawkins founded EA right here in San Mateo in 1982.
Snowflake
The data company was founded in San Mateo in 2012.
Napster
The file-sharing pioneer set up shop near 4th Avenue back in 1999.

Headquartered here today

PlayStation
Sony Interactive Entertainment runs the global PlayStation business from Bridgepointe.
Roblox
Headquartered right over in Bay Meadows.
GoPro
Moved its headquarters to San Mateo in 2012.
Verkada, Coupa & Guidewire
All three call San Mateo home right now.

Not bad for a town most people just drive through on the way to somewhere else.
