New in San Mateo: Summer 2026

Updated July 1, 2026

A downtown brunch boom, a matcha bar, Korean BBQ, and more — the freshest spots to open around town.

San Mateo's food scene has been on a tear this year, especially downtown. Here are the newest places worth checking out, all opened in roughly the last year.

Eat & Drink

Whisper

Asian fusion brunch · Downtown, Baldwin Ave · opened January 2026

A former Sweet Maple chef turned the old Wursthall space into a 140-seat brunch spot with Korean-leaning plates like galbi eggs Benedict and gochujang cioppino.

Reposado

Mexican fine dining · Downtown, Baldwin Ave · opened February 2026

The Palo Alto favorite's second location brings regional Mexican cooking from Oaxaca and the Yucatan downtown, with a deep tequila list.

Johnny's

Breakfast & brunch · Downtown, B St · opened March 2026

The Half Moon Bay brunch destination from the It's Italia team moved its full menu into the Brickline building with a bigger dining room.

MIXT

Salads & bowls · Downtown, B St · opened March 2026

The San Francisco salad chain landed in the former B Street Books, building make-your-own bowls and signature salads daily until 9pm.

Izumi Matcha

Matcha cafe · Downtown, E 3rd Ave · opened early 2026

A premium Japanese matcha bar pouring ceremonial-grade lattes and matcha drinks in the heart of downtown.

OLHSO House

Korean BBQ · Downtown, B St · opened 2025

A Korean master chef's first U.S. restaurant, doing tableside grilling of premium cuts plus Busan-inspired hot pots.

The Breakfast Club at Midtown

Brunch · Bay Meadows · opened October 2025

The San Jose brunch cult favorite opened its first Peninsula spot at Bay Meadows with a big patio and a full bar.

Bahche Mezzehouse & Wine Bar

Mediterranean & wine bar · Bay Meadows · opened August 2025

A family-run mezze house in the old Roam Artisan Burgers space, pairing Turkish and Mediterranean small plates with a 60-plus bottle wine list.

Sama Coffee Shop

Middle Eastern coffee · Downtown, S B St · opened 2025

An Iraqi-Palestinian coffee house serving sand-brewed Turkish coffee, cardamom lattes, and Middle Eastern sweets in a backgammon-friendly space.

Shops

Honey & Hive

Kids' clothing boutique · Downtown, E 3rd Ave · opened May 2025

A downtown boutique selling ethically made baby and children's clothing crafted from premium Turkish cotton.

Akiba HQ

Anime & hobby shop · Downtown, E 3rd Ave · opened Fall 2025

A collector's shop stocked with Gunpla model kits, direct-import anime figures, gashapon, and trading cards.
