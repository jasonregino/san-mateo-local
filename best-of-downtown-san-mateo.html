Best of Downtown San Mateo

Updated Updated July 2, 2026

A walkable tour of the downtown core, from a Michelin sushi counter to the bakery everyone lines up for.

Downtown San Mateo, the walkable stretch around B Street and the old train depot, packs a lot into a few blocks. Here's a tour, from destination sushi to a cult cookie shop.

Eat & drink

Sushi Yoshizumi

Michelin omakase · 4th Ave

An intimate Edomae omakase counter with a longtime spot in the Michelin Guide. The special-occasion sushi destination.

Sushi Maruyama

Omakase · Baldwin Ave

A tiny, reservations-only omakase bar serving pristine nigiri just off the downtown core. The hidden-gem counter.

Pausa Bar & Cookery

Italian + Neapolitan pizza · 4th Ave

From-scratch Veneto pasta, wood-fired pizza, and house-cured salumi. A Michelin Bib Gourmand pick and the downtown anchor.

Whisper

Korean-inspired brunch · Baldwin Ave

Korean-inspired brunch from a former Sweet Maple chef, opened early 2026 in the old Wursthall space. The buzzy new arrival.

Johnny's

All-day diner brunch · Brickline (B St)

The Half Moon Bay diner favorite that brought its all-day breakfast to the Brickline on B Street in 2026.

Fogbird

Craft cocktails · S B St

A craft cocktail lounge with soaring ceilings, exposed brick, and a nine-foot bar. The date-night spot.

B Street & Vine

Wine bar + patio · S B St

A long-running neighborhood wine bar known for bruschetta boards and a heated patio. The low-key one.

Coffee & sweets

Three Bees Coffee

Corner coffeehouse since 2002 · 3rd & B

A family-owned corner coffeehouse at 3rd and B, open since 2002. The local's go-to.

Backhaus

German bakery · 3rd Ave

A women-owned German-inspired bakery famous for naturally leavened sourdough and pretzel croissants. The destination bakery.

Izumi Matcha

Matcha bar · 3rd Ave

A premium Japanese matcha bar for ceremonial-grade lattes and matcha soft serve.

Antoine's Cookie Shop

Warm cookies · 2nd Ave

A beloved little shop for warm, soft-baked cookies from family recipes, run by Antoine himself. A hidden-gem sweet stop.

Shops

Suruki Supermarket

Japanese grocery · 4th Ave

A longtime Japanese grocer with a fresh fish counter, prepared bento, and hard-to-find imports.

Therapy Stores

Gift boutique · 4th Ave

A playful boutique packed with gifts, home goods, cards, and offbeat finds. The browse-and-gift stop.

Scandinavian Designs

Modern furniture · S B St

An airy showroom of modern Scandinavian furniture and home decor right on B Street.

Atlas Skateboarding

Skate shop · 2nd Ave

An independent skate shop with boards, gear, and a real neighborhood-scene vibe.

That's the downtown loop. Know a spot we walked past? Tell us at hello@sanmateolocal.com.
