Good to know

Questions from business owners

Is getting listed really free?

Yes. A basic listing is free, no strings. If you run a real San Mateo business, we want you on the guide. Send it to us with the form above and we'll add it.

What's the difference between a free listing and a Featured Listing?

A free listing puts you in the guide where locals can find you. A Featured Listing makes you stand out: the orange Featured badge, the top of your category, and a place in the Featured strip on our homepage.

What exactly do I get with a Featured Listing?

The Featured badge on your listing, top position on your category page, and a spot in the rotating Featured strip on the homepage. When someone's browsing for what you offer, you're one of the first things they see.

Is it monthly or one-time?

Your choice. Go monthly at $39 a month to stay featured for as long as you like (cancel anytime), or pay $49 once for a single 30-day run. Same placement either way. Monthly is the better deal if you plan to stick around.

What's the $149 Business Spotlight?

Everything in a Featured Listing, plus two things that keep working long-term: a full editorial story about your business on its own page (Google can find it for years), and a big feature card up top on the homepage. It's a one-time payment, and the story page stays up for good.

Do you write the story, or do I?

We write it, with you. We'll ask a few quick questions, put together a draft, and send it to you to approve before it goes live. Your story, in your words, just polished.

How many featured slots are there?

We keep it to six at a time, on purpose. If everyone's featured, no one stands out, so we cap it. When all six are taken, we start a waitlist and let you know when one opens up.

How long until my Featured Listing goes live?

Usually a day or two after payment. We add you by hand (that's the point of a curated guide), so it's not instant, but it's quick.

What happens if I cancel, or when my 30 days end?

Your placement comes down and you go back to a normal free listing, still in the guide. Nothing disappears. With the $149, your story page stays up permanently either way.

Can I fix a mistake in my listing?

Yes. Use the "Is this your business?" link on your listing or just email us, and we'll update it. Free listings included.
